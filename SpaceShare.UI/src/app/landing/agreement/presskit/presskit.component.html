Welcome to Space Share!

This press kit provides information about Space Share, a website dedicated to simplifying affordable apartment hunting in the National Capital Region (NCR) of the Philippines.

About Space Share

Founded in 2024 by VRZN Industries Ltd., Space Share is a user-friendly platform connecting renters and landlords directly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make finding an affordable apartment in the NCR effortless. We achieve this by:

  • Prioritizing budget-friendly listings.
  • Offering a user-friendly platform for both renters and landlords.
  • Fostering a community focused on positive interaction.

Why Space Share is in the News

Space Share is revolutionizing the NCR rental market by making affordable apartment hunting a breeze through its user-friendly platform.
